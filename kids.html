Bread Cutter & Sealer

Make fun, sealed sandwiches in cute shapes — perfect for kids' lunches, picnics, and creative snacks.

How to Use

  1. Prepare: Add filling (jam, peanut butter, tuna, cheese, egg, etc.) to one bread slice, keeping 1–2 cm from edges.
  2. Top: Place the second slice on top, aligning edges.
  3. Cut: Position cutter in the center and press firmly.
  4. Seal: If built-in, press to crimp edges; if two-piece, cut first then seal.
  5. Serve: Lift, remove, and trim crust if desired.
  6. Clean: Wash with warm soapy water and dry before storing.
